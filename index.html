Cat Facts

A cat can sprint at about 31 miles per hour, which is great for catching prey and chasing laser pointers!
Cats sleep an average of 16 hours a day and spend 1/3 of their awake time grooming themselves.
A cat's nose print is unique, much like a human's fingerprint.
Cats have over 20 different vocalizations, including meowing, purring, trilling, chirping, and growling.
